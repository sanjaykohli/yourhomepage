1. What You're Buying

When you purchase pixels on YourHomepage.in, you're buying the exclusive right to display your content on those specific pixels for 99 years from the date of purchase. Think of it as leasing a billboard, but for nearly a century!

Your pixels will remain active and displaying your chosen content for 99 years. We handle all hosting, maintenance, and technical upkeep. You pay once, and we take care of everything else for the entire duration.

3. Payment & Refunds

All pixel purchases are final and non-refundable. Why? Because once you buy pixels, they're reserved exclusively for you for 99 years. We recommend:

Your pixels can display a solid color or an image, along with a link to your website. However, your content must be:

We reserve the right to remove content that violates our guidelines without refund. This includes illegal content, malware, inappropriate material, or anything that could harm our platform or other users.
